# Spring 2026 Pebble App Contest

Source: https://repebble.com/blog/spring-2026-pebble-app-contest
Saved: 2026-04-05

## Timeline

- Duration: April 2-19, 2026 UTC
- Results: Announced a few days after April 19

## Prize Structure (25 Total Watches)

### Developer Draw (10 watches)
Random selection from developers who import/claim pre-2025 apps or publish new apps for Time 2/Round 2. Maximum 2 entries per developer.

### Most Hearts (10 watches)
Split into two phases:
- 5 watches for top 5 hearted apps created April 2-12
- 5 watches for top 5 hearted apps created April 13-20

### Pebble Team Judging (3 transparent Time 2 watches)
Evaluated on: **creativity, cleverness, good use of new platforms, and design.**
Eligible apps: created since January 1, 2026 (broader window than other categories).

### Heart an App Draw (2 watches)
Two random winners who heart contest apps.

## Eligibility
- Apps must be created on or after April 2, 2026 (except team judging: since Jan 1, 2026)
- International shipping available

## Rules
- Winners choose Time 2 or Round 2
- Frivolous, fake, low-value, or scam submissions will be removed
- Maximum 2 entries per developer for the draw
