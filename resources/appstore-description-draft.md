# Steady. Your wrist, always in the know.
## Introduction
Steady is a feature-rich watchface for Pebble Time 2 that brings health data and daily essentials together in one glanceable display. Glucose levels, heart rate, steps, weather and battery status: everything that matters, readable at a glance.
## Description
Built with design-system precision for the 64-color e-paper screen, Steady raises the bar on what a Pebble watchface can look and feel like. Color-coded glucose zones, a 3-hour sparkline graph, and clean information hierarchy make it as functional as it is polished.
Supports Nightscout and Dexcom Share. Designed for Pebble Time 2 (Emery) and Pebble Round 2 (Gabbro). Built for people who live with diabetes and anyone who simply wants their wrist to do more.
